Hard Boiled Eggs

A classic dish simple to make edible alone or as additional pieces to a whole dish

Ingredients

  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Place eggs in medium-sized pot
  2. Add water up to an inch above the eggss
  3. Bring water to a boil and then turn off the heat
  4. Cover the pot and leave for 9-12 minutes
  5. Transfer eggs to a bowl of ice water and let them sit for 14 minutes
  6. Peel shell
