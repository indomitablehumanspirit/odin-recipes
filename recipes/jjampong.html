Jjampong

A Korean Chinese dish with a mix of many sea food options along with noodles and a spicy soup

Ingredients

Soup Stock

  • Dried Anchovies (1 oz with head and guts removed)
  • Dried Kelp (6x6 piece)
  • 12 Cups of Water

Vegetables

  • Green Onions(4 cut into 2 in lengths)
  • Leeks (2 oz cut into 1/2 by 2 in strips)
  • Bok Choy (4 oz)
  • Cabbage Leaves (3 oz cut into bite-size pieces)
  • Onion (4 oz)
  • Small Carrot (peeled and cut into 2-inch strips)

Seafood and Meat

  • Mussels (8 pieces)
  • Large Shrimp (4 pieces)
  • Squid (4 oz)
  • Small Clams (24 pieces)
  • Thin-Sliced Beef (4 oz)

Noodles and Seasoning

  • Jjajangmyeon Noodles (1 lb)
  • Gochugaru (5 tbsp)
  • Toasted Sesame Oil (1 tsp)
  • Minced Garlic (1 tsp)
  • Vegatble Oil (1/4 cup)
  • Fish Sauce (1 tbsp)
  • Kosher Salt (1tsp)

Instructions

Prep protein

  1. Scrub, debeard, and soak mussels in salt water for a few hours
  2. Shell and devein shrimp
  3. Remove guts and slice squid into rings
  4. Soak clams in salt water for a few hours
  5. Cut Beef into bite-size pieces

Make Stock

  1. Combine soup stock ingredients into large pot
  2. Cover and cook over medium-high for 20 minutes
  3. Reduce heat to low and cook for 20 more minutes
  4. Strain stock to remove anchovies and kelp

Make hot pepper flake mixture

  1. Combine 2 tbsp of hot pepper flakes and 1 tsp of toasted sesame oil in a bowl
  2. Mix well and set aside

Make jjampong

  1. Heat a large wok over high heat
  2. Add vegetable oil, garlic,ginger
  3. Stir fwith a wooden sppon, until garlic is crispy (about 20 seconds)
  4. Add beef and stir until slightly cooked
  5. Push ingredients over to one side of the wok
  6. Tilt pot so vegetable oil is on the opposite side and add 3 tbsp of hot peper flakes
  7. Stir and mix flakes and oil for (about 1 minute)
  8. Add green onion, leek, cabbage and stir until vegetables are wilted (3-4 minutes)
  9. Add 6 cups of soup stock and all seafoods as well as bok choy
  10. Cover and cook for 7-8 minutes until mussels and clams are open
  11. Stir in hot pepper flakes mixture from earlier as well as the fish sauce and kosher salt
  12. Cover and let simmer over low heat

Noodles

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil
  2. Add noodles and stir
  3. Cover and cook until tender but still chewy (5-8 minutes)
  4. Strain and rinse the noodles in cold running water

Final Steps

  1. Heat soup over high heat
  2. Divide noodles in seperate serving bowls
  3. Add soup as well as everything else over top of the noodles
